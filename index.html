About VWELDCORE

VWELDCORE INNOVATIONS NG LIMITED (RC 8909233) — Founded 2025, incorporated 9th October 2025 under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020. A dual‑expertise engineering firm combining precision metal fabrication with cold chain specialisation.

We serve hospitality, food processing, pharmaceutical, retail, and industrial clients across Nigeria. From small restaurant chillers to large‑scale warehouse cold storage, we combine precision engineering, quality materials, and a relentless commitment to reliability — ensuring every project is delivered on time and functions flawlessly. Fully compliant with Nigerian Industrial Standards and NAFDAC‑compliant fabrication for food and pharmaceutical applications.

Our Services

Full‑service metal fabrication and cold chain solutions for commercial, industrial, and institutional clients.

Cold Room Fabrication & Installation

Design, fabrication, and complete installation of walk‑in cold rooms for hospitality, food processing, pharmaceutical, and retail clients across Nigeria.

Blast Freezer Solutions

Industrial‑grade blast freezers engineered to your temperature specifications, from small restaurant units to large‑scale commercial freezing systems.

Stainless Steel Fabrication

Custom stainless steel shelves, work tables, kitchen equipment, and commercial fittings built to the highest hygiene and durability standards.

General Metal Fabrication

Gates, railings, staircases, security doors, structural steel, and architectural metalwork for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Welding & Repair Services

ARC, MIG/TIG, and spot welding for fabrication and on‑site repair. Emergency call‑out available for critical equipment breakdowns.

Manufacturing & Supply

Manufacture and sale of metal products: storage tanks, frames, brackets, and custom industrial components. Bulk orders welcome.
